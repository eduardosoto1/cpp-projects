# Pomodoro Timer

## What is it?
This is a pomodoro timer based off the pomodoro techinque. I created this to help me manage my time during my studies. 

## How Does it work
They work in twenty-five minute long intervals which you put your focus on your task at hand. Once it is over, take a five minute break. After four intervals/pomodoros, take a break of 15-30 minutes.

## What benefits does the pomodoro timer offer?
- Enhances focuses on task
- Reduces mental fatigue
- Combats procasination
